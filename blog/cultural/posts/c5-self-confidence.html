Self Confidence

What is the relationship between Mindfulness and Self Awareness?

They go hand in hand, even to a point where they could be something.

What was your experience of it? how did you feel?

I tried both self-awareness practices, and I found the Body Scan more calming whereas I found the ‘Journaling’ difficult due to my writing skills not being able to keep up with my brain. I spent most the time trying to remember how to spell words and the like. But I will keep both practices up in the hope I seeing more results.

What did you take away from The Chapter?

I want to learn more about being self-aware to the point of being able to control my self-confidence. To be mindful and confident at the same time. To be able to be able to put people at ease around me and to be committable around me. More personal I guess, to be less shy.
