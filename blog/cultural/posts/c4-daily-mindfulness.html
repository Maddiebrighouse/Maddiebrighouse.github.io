What did you learn from your 'mindfulness conversation'? How did the other person find it?

The other person told me much more than they meant to because I was so attentive and personable.The conversation quickly turned into a deep and meaningful talk. I found the experience very calming, and I remembered more about the interview.

Which of the three suggested techniques will you use to sustain your practice?

Listening Looping: Going back and forth to make sure you understand what the speaker said. Dipping: Checking in with ourselves. I found Listening the most benefits to me as I often interrupted the speaker to add to the conversation of to direct it.
