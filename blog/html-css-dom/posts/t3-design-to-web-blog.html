What a responsive site is, and why responsiveness is important?

A Responsive site allows a website to change size, say from a desktop to a phone size and all that's in between. While still being usable elegant and allowing usability on any size screen.

What mobile first design is, and why it's important?

In 2016 a study showed that the internet was being viewed on more mobiles and tablets than desktops. Designing for mobile first allows you to include the necessity to create a user-friendly site. As your canvas (desktop) gets bigger, you can expand your design and fluff it up with stuff, like ads. Image trying to compress a massive site like NZ Herald down to a mobile site, that's a bit job. It's somewhat logical to start small and build out bigger.

What a wireframe is and why we use it?

A Wireframe is a sketch of the design of your website, A visual representation of the element on your site. It lets you map out where essential elements go. It allows easier communication with the client, to be able to show them what you are going to do and why.
